A Modern Solution for Juror and Small-Payment Disbursements

Our goal is to become the leading juror payment platform in the U.S., replacing outdated systems that rely on mailing physical checks. Our solution also supports other institutions and their payees by eliminating the inefficiencies of printing, mailing, and cashing paper checks.

The Hidden Costs of Paper Checks

Many counties and businesses still rely on physical checks to make small payments. This outdated method results in high administrative costs and increased fraud risk for payors. For payees, it causes delays, travel expenses, and overall inconvenience.

Uncovering the Pain Points: Insights from User Interviews

Direct feedback from users revealed key challenges in B2C payments, validating the need for VerityPay's streamlined solution.

Through in-depth user interviews with businesses and consumers involved in B2C transactions, we gained valuable insight into the challenges faced in managing and receiving payments. These conversations revealed frustration with outdated systems, lack of transparency, and inefficient communication—echoing findings from our competitor analysis. This feedback confirmed a clear opportunity for VerityPay to innovate and streamline the B2C payment experience.

Payment Delays Impact Business

"We lose 2-3 days of productivity waiting for checks to clear. Our cash flow suffers significantly."

- Small Business Owner

Mobile Access is Essential

"I need to check payment status on my phone while traveling. Current systems are impossible to use on mobile."

- Freelance Consultant

Security is a Major Concern

"We've had two instances of check fraud in the past year. Digital payments would give us better control and security."

- Financial Controller

Communication is Inefficient

- Accounts Payable Manager
