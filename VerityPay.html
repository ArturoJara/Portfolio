A Modern Solution for Juror and Small-Payment Disbursements

Our goal is to become the leading juror payment platform in the U.S., replacing outdated systems that rely on mailing physical checks. Our solution also supports other institutions and their payees by eliminating the inefficiencies of printing, mailing, and cashing paper checks.

The Hidden Costs of Paper Checks

Many counties and businesses still rely on physical checks to make small payments. This outdated method results in high administrative costs and increased fraud risk for payors. For payees, it causes delays, travel expenses, and overall inconvenience.

Revolutionizing B2C Payments with a

User Interviews

Inverview Results

10 professionals (ages 22–50) working in payment processing roles across industries such as tech, br logistics and finance. The problem lies in operational inefficiency.

Companies often stick to legacy systems that are slow, fragmented, and frustrating for both them and their customers. VerityPay addresses this by radically simplifying the B2C payment process, enabling faster, smoother, and more reliable transactions that build trust and loyalty.

Pain Points

  • Payment delays: Processing payments manually (checks, bank deposits, portals) is slow and creates frustration for both companies and customers.
  • High processing costs: Sending physical checks, using intermediaries, or complex payment portals increases expenses.
  • Consumer friction: Many payment methods require creating accounts, downloading apps, or completing complicated registrations, lowering acceptance rates.
  • Human errors: Incorrectly entered bank or portal data causes rejections, returns, and costly reprocessing.
  • Reconciliation issues: Manually tracking who has been paid is time-consuming, inaccurate, and requires heavy follow-up.
  • Lack of visibility: Not knowing in real time if the customer received and accepted the payment creates uncertainty.
  • Security concerns: Transferring money digitally always raises doubts about privacy and data protection.

User Preferences

  • Extreme simplicity: Users value fast, frictionless payment processes without complex registration or app downloads.
  • No friction for consumers: Payment systems should not require account creation or app downloads from the end user.
  • Top-notch security: Ensuring that the platform meets high security standards to protect user data is a priority.
  • Clear and low-cost transactions: Transparent, simple transactions without hidden fees are highly preferred.
  • Instant confirmations: Real-time confirmations that the payment was received and accepted are essential for users.
  • Easy integration: The system should easily integrate with existing business tools (ERP, CRM, accounting software).
  • Access to detailed reports: Users desire a system that provides clear, downloadable payment and reconciliation reports.

Oportunities

  • Eliminate adoption barriers: By offering a frictionless SMS payment system, VerityPay could greatly increase the adoption of digital payments among users who have previously relied on manual methods.
  • Reduce processing costs: Replacing paper checks and intermediaries with SMS payments allows businesses to save on transaction and processing fees.
  • Enhance user experience: A simplified, user-friendly payment process leads to higher acceptance and satisfaction from both payors and payees.
  • Increase payment acceptance rates: With SMS-based payments, consumers who may have previously been hesitant can more easily receive and accept payments.
  • Gain competitive advantage: Companies that can process payments quickly and with minimal friction can build customer loyalty and outperform competitors.
  • Provide real-time payment visibility: With live tracking and automatic reconciliation, businesses can manage payments more efficiently and without delay.
  • Offer easy integration: VerityPay could integrate seamlessly with existing business software, reducing the learning curve and increasing efficiency.

Next Steps

VerityPay has been a powerful opportunity to reimagine how disbursements — especially for civic duties like jury service — can be more efficient, secure, and user-friendly. The research phase uncovered significant pain points in outdated processes, and the MVP was shaped around directly solving those core inefficiencies.

Key Learnings

  • Speed and simplicity are top priorities — users greatly value fast, streamlined payment delivery over complex options.
  • Trust and transparency are essential — users want clear, timely updates on how and when their payments are processed.

Next Step

The next step for VerityPay is to move beyond the MVP and validate the product in a live environment. By tracking metrics such as digital disbursement rates, issue resolution time, and user satisfaction scores, the team can measure impact and drive continuous improvement.

Future Improvements

  • Multilingual support to increase accessibility.
  • Real-time notifications to improve transparency and engagement.
  • Integration with other civic systems for broader government use cases.
  • Personal dashboards for users to track and manage all civic-related transactions.

Ultimately, the success of VerityPay will depend on close collaboration with users and institutions, ensuring that the system grows in a direction that serves both efficiency and public trust.
