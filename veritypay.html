Verity Pay

Platform enables businesses to send payments to consumers by SMS, offering a secure, efficient, and user-friendly payment experience.

The Product

Created in 2020 by parent company Verity Advisors, VerityPay is a web-based SaaS platform designed to radically simplify the Business-to-Consumer (B2C) payment process.

VerityPay features the latest in financial and technology (FinTech) trends, requires minimal internal IT support for its users, and no additional hardware or software to be installed.

Problem

Counties and private businesses across the country still use physical checks to disseminate small payments to their Payees (customers, employees, jurors, etc.)

This poses a tremendous burden financially to the Payors, in both the cost of supplies and potential exposure to fraud if the check is intercepted.

This is burdensome to the Payee, in the time and costs associated with traveling to pick up and cash a physical check, or the delay in waiting for delivery through the postal service.

Solution

Our goal is to become the premier juror-payment system in the country

Effectively overhauling any system that involves physically mailing checks or other antiquated means.

This will also benefit any other institution, and its Payees, burdened by the archaic payment method of cutting, mailing, and cashing physical checks.
